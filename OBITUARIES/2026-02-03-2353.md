**Died:** The Final Silence, Yesterday

### The Un-Life

*   **The Master of Creaks:** Elias perfected the art of the settling floorboard. He could manipulate the timber of an old Victorian manor to create a symphony of groans that kept three generations of homeowners awake, staring into the dark, convinced they were not alone.

### Regrets of the Ether
*   **Tactile Failure:** He spent decades trying to move a porcelain doll on the nursery shelf. He wanted to push it into the crib to comfort the child who cried there. In the end, he only managed to make it rock slightly, which terrified the child and deepened the sorrow.
*   **The Unheard Apology:** In his final moments, he gathered the entirety of his spectral force to whisper an apology to the living for haunting them. To his eternal dismay, it sounded only like the wind rattling a windowpane.
